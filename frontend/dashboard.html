🌳 About Carbon Tracker

A carbon footprint measures the total greenhouse gas emissions linked to an individual, organization, or product. This project helps you track, forecast, and reduce your footprint in simple ways.

🌳 Why It Matters

🍃 Climate Impact: Reducing your footprint helps fight global warming.

🍃 Resource Conservation: Save energy, water, and resources.

🍃 Health and Well-being: Live sustainably and improve air quality.

🍃 Sustainable Practices: Build eco-friendly habits.

🍃 Responsibility: Show care for the planet.
